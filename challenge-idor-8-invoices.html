Security Advisory

                
Invoice IDs follow a structured format: INV-[YEAR]-[NUMBER]. The API endpoint does not verify authorization before returning invoice data. Confidential invoices may have specific number patterns.

            


            

                
System Notes

                
Understand the numbering pattern and modify the last digits to access restricted invoices. Internal or confidential invoices may use special number ranges.
