Security Advisory

                
Projects are grouped by team ID. Users can only access projects belonging to their team. The API endpoint accepts team_id parameters without verifying team membership.

            


            

                
System Notes

                
Team IDs are sequential integers starting from 1. Administrative workspaces may be associated with low-numbered team IDs. Access requires discovering your team_id first.
